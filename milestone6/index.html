Who is Reel Boy?

Jayson Haslambrock, bassist and songwriter for other Utah indie rock outfits, set out on his own to make music more faithful to his own influences under the moniker “Reel Boy”.

Minimalist chord progressions, poppy melodies, and a couple of fuzz pedals combine to make the sound that makes Reel Boy what it is.
